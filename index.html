Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Arc Raiders Ops ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the application.

Information We Collect

The App does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

However, the App uses Firebase services provided by Google, which may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs
  • Usage statistics
  • Approximate location (country-level, derived from IP address)

This information is used strictly for app performance monitoring, analytics, and improving user experience.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy

External Links and APIs

The App may connect to third-party APIs and may open external websites in your device's browser. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services.

Advertising

The App does not currently display third-party advertisements. Advertising services may be integrated in future versions of the App. If this occurs, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect the information processed by the App. Data transmitted through Firebase services is encrypted in transit.

Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.
